Cluster Head – LIC

Chennai•Full-time•2•Negotiable (based on CV)

Overview

We are seeking a dynamic and results-driven Cluster Head to lead LIC operations across a designated cluster. This role requires strong leadership, strategic thinking, and the ability to drive business growth while ensuring compliance with organizational standards.

Key Responsibilities

  • Lead and manage a cluster of LIC branches/units to achieve sales and revenue targets
  • Develop and implement strategies to expand market presence and customer base
  • Supervise and mentor branch managers, agents, and sales teams to maximize productivity
  • Ensure compliance with LIC policies, regulatory guidelines, and ethical standards
  • Monitor performance metrics, prepare reports, and present insights to senior management
  • Drive customer satisfaction through quality service delivery and relationship management
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to support product launches and marketing initiatives

Requirements

  • Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration, Finance, or related field (MBA preferred)
  • 2–5 years of experience in insurance, financial services, or sales leadership
  • Strong knowledge of life insurance products, distribution channels, and regulatory framework
  • Excellent communication, negotiation, and interpersonal skills
  • Proven ability to lead teams, manage operations, and deliver results under pressure
  • Analytical mindset with proficiency in reporting and performance tracking

Benefits

  • Competitive salary package with performance-based incentives
  • Health and life insurance coverage
  • Professional development and training opportunities
  • Career growth within India’s leading life insurance organization
  • Supportive work environment with emphasis on teamwork and innovation
